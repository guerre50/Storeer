Micro visual story telling in 5 frames

1. Think a plot for your storee.

2. Gather all you need to produce it: friends, assets, place, ...

3. Take the pictures

4. Once finished, upload them to Storeer and share it with the community

5. Let the Storees
begin

Challenge your creativity making short stories out of 5 pictures. If you already acquired a level 90000 of food and pet photography you can try out storee-telling leveling.
Explore the Storees created by the community and let other's awesomeness surprise you. Every week you will receive a set of badges that you can give to others to express your amazement, gratitude or appreciation.
Storees are inspired by yonkoma (yon - four and koma - frame), a four-panel Japanese comic strip that follows the structure known as Kishōtenketsu:

  • Ki: set the scene
  • shō: develop the story
  • ten: climax setup
  • ketsu: conclusion
